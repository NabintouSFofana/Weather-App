Bring your own key.

This is a static, no-backend weather app. To respect rate limits and keep secrets out of public code, you bring your own free OpenWeatherMap API key. It saves to this browser only — never sent anywhere except OpenWeather itself.

Need a free key? Sign up at OpenWeatherMap ↗

⌗ Honest note: the free tier gives you 60 requests per minute. The key is stored in this browser's localStorage only — nothing leaves your machine except calls to openweathermap.org.
